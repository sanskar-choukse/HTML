    UIT-RGPV UG Admission

    UIT-RGPV is one of the premier engineering institutions in Central India. The Institution was established in the year 1986 by the Government of Madhya Pradesh as the Government Engineering College (GEC), Bhopal. In 1998 the Government of Madhya Pradesh declared this institute autonomous and named it as Bhopal Engineering College.

    Read More

    University Polytechnic

    UIT-RGPV is one of the premier engineering institutions in Central India. The Institution was established in the year 1986 by the Government of Madhya Pradesh as the Government Engineering College (GEC), Bhopal.

    Read More

    University Dual Degree Integrated PG Program

    This university is established in the year 1998, by Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Act 13, 1998. Over a sprawling Campus of about 247 acres, the RGPV is marching towards development into a center of excellence in the arena of Technical Education, Research and Innovations.

    Read More

    About School of Pharmaceutical Science

    One of the front line institutions in Madhya Pradesh dedicated entirely for post graduate study and research in Pharmaceutical Sciences and allied disciplines, was established on 28th Dec. 2002.

    Read More

    About School of Information Technology

    The School of Information Technology at RGTU was established in 2002-03. School of Information Technology offers B Tech Computer Science and Business Systems as well as M. Tech. in Information Technology and M Tech in Data Sciences.

    Read More

    About School of Energy & Environment Management

    With the view to strengthen the Nations resolve to achieve Energy Security and Environmental Sustainability in the foreseeable future, RGPV established a UTD in the area of Energy Technology and Environmental Management in the year 2002 with the prime objective to focus on Green Energy Technologies.

    Read More

    About School of Bio-Molecular Engineering & Biotechnology

    School of Biotechnology at RGPV was established in 2003 with a mission to build up state of the art infrastructure and facilities for world class research and development and balanced innovative teaching programmes commensurate with the needs of the nation and Madhya Pradesh, in particular.

    Read More

    About School of Nano Technology

    The RGPV, School of Nanotechnology was established in 2007, the School of Nanotechnology is currently running M.Tech (Nanotechnology) and Ph D program in addition to cutting edge research and development in areas of high relevance to the country which includes Nano-materials, functional materials and thin films.

    Read More
